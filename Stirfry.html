Alex's Stirfry

This is for when there are too many leftovers in the fridge, this technique requires a lot of patients, precision and effort. So the base idea is take all the leftovers in the firdge and throw it all into one, its actually really simple and you'd be amazed how good it is.

Ingredients

  • Anything leftover in the fridge

Steps

  1. Take all ingredients out of fridge
  2. Open all conatiners
  3. Butter your cast iron pan
  4. Throw everything in depending on cook time
  5. Top with cheese
  6. Soak in all the compliments
